Some information about the country of Germany!

The country of Germany is one of the Europe's largest countries, and the country of Germany encompasses a huge variety of of landscapes such as the tall, the sheer mountains of the south; the sandy, rolling plains of the north; and the plains of the agricultural east overall. The east-central city of Berlin which still has a phoenixlike environment from the ashes of World War II. Germany is a member of the European Union, one-third of Germany is still covered with forests and woodlands. Germany is also a parliamentary and federal democracy. An important fact is that all higher public institutions such as private and public universities in Germany are free (even for non-Germans). Germany, Switzerland, Austria, Luxembourg and Liechtenstein have German as the official language.
