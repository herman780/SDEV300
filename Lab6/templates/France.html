Some information about the country of France!

France is a country of Northwestern Europe and it is considered historically and culturally wise to be the most important nations in the entirety of the Western world. The country of France is bordered by the Atlantic Ocean and the Mediterranean Sea, and the Alps and Pyrenees. It is Europe's most crucial and important agricultural producer of a lot of agricultural resources and is one of the world's leading industrial powers.
