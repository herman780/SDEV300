Some information about the country of Spain!

The country of Spain is filled of castles, snowcapped mountains, and sophisticated cities, and much more. It is made a favoured travel destination for most travelers and explorers. Spain has a Parliamentary monarchy form of government. Spain occupies the area of most of the Iberian Peninsula and stretching south from the Pyrenees Mountains to the Strait of Gibraltar, which overall separates Spain from Africa. Spain is bordered to the west by Portugal; to the northeast it borders France.
